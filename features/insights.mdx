## Overview

Wovly's Smart Insights automatically analyzes your messages across all connected platforms (Gmail, Slack, iMessage, custom websites, etc.) to surface important information you might have missed. Every hour, the system cross-references new messages with your profile, goals, calendar, and historical context to generate actionable insights.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Message Collection (Every Hour)">
    Collect new messages from Gmail, Slack, iMessage, custom websites, and more
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fact Extraction">
    AI extracts key facts: people, dates, commitments, questions, sentiment
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cross-Referencing">
    Compare facts against your profile, calendar, goals, and conversation history
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insight Generation">
    When conflicts, gaps, or patterns detected ‚Üí generate insight
  </Step>
  <Step title="Priority Scoring (1-5)">
    Score based on urgency, sender importance, time sensitivity, keywords
  </Step>
  <Step title="Smart Filtering">
    Show only high-value insights aligned with your goals
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
You'll typically see **3-10 insights per day** instead of hundreds of messages.
</Info>

## Priority Scoring

Each insight receives a priority score from 1-5:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Priority 5 (Critical)" icon="5">
    - Urgent conflicts or missed deadlines
    - Important unanswered questions
    - Scheduling conflicts
    - Pending responses from VIPs

    **Action:** Address immediately
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Priority 4 (High)" icon="4">
    - Scheduling conflicts
    - Pending responses from VIPs
    - Important but not urgent items

    **Action:** Address today
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Priority 3 (Medium)" icon="3">
    - General follow-ups
    - Minor conflicts
    - Background information

    **Action:** Review when convenient
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Priority 2 (Low)" icon="2">
    - FYI items
    - Nice-to-know information

    **Action:** Optional review
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Priority 1 (Minimal)" icon="1">
    - Archival facts
    - Low-priority updates

    **Action:** Usually auto-filtered
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Multi-Source Analysis

Insights can combine information from multiple platforms:

<CodeGroup>

```text Example Scenario
1. Client sends email asking for meeting
2. Colleague messages on Slack about client prep
3. Calendar shows conflict at requested time
4. Daycare sends message about early pickup needed

Generated insight (Priority 5):
"Client meeting conflicts with existing event.
Early daycare pickup at 4pm limits afternoon.
Colleague waiting for prep discussion."

Suggested action: Propose alternative time,
coordinate with colleague, arrange daycare backup.
```

</CodeGroup>

This holistic view saves you from context-switching across apps.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No insights showing">
    **Possible causes:**
    - No new messages in the past hour
    - All insights below priority filter
    - Integration not configured

    **Solutions:**
    - Check Settings ‚Üí Integrations
    - Lower priority filter threshold
    - Manually trigger refresh
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Too many insights">
    **Solutions:**
    - Increase priority threshold to 3 or 4
    - Add more specific goals
    - Configure per-platform filters
    - Exclude low-value channels
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Insights seem inaccurate">
    **Solutions:**
    - Update profile in Settings ‚Üí About Me
    - Connect Google Calendar
    - Give system 2-3 days to build context
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Performance

<Warning>
**Data Processing:**
- Analysis happens on your machine
- Insights stored locally (90-day retention)
- Only message snippets sent to LLM
- Full content never leaves your device
</Warning>

**Performance Metrics:**
- Average: 30-60 seconds for 50 messages
- LLM calls: 2-3 per batch
- Token usage: ~5,000-10,000 per hour
- Cost: $0.01-0.03/hour with Claude Sonnet
